About the Committee

The Operation: Becoming One Planning Committee was established to provide strategic oversight, encouragement and accountability throughout the groom's journey toward marriage. The committee operates with professionalism, confidentiality and excellence while maintaining one core principle: No meeting shall proceed before food has been discussed.

Committee Objectives

Mission

To guide and support the groom through every stage of preparation until the successful completion of Operation: Becoming One.

Vision

To become the most effective wedding planning committee ever assembled for a wedding that has not yet been scheduled.

Terms of Reference

  • Provide guidance to the groom.
  • Monitor overall progress.
  • Maintain confidentiality.
  • Encourage continuous personal development.
  • Review future wedding logistics.
  • Approve catering decisions.
  • Evaluate dessert options.
  • Inspect cake quality.
  • Protect committee traditions.
  • Ensure every meeting includes refreshments.
